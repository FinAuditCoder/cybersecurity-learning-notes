# Careers in Cybersecurity
## Overview
This room introduced different career paths in cybersecurity and the roles within the industry.
## Roles I Learned About
- Security Operations Centre Analyst
- Penetration Tester
- Security Engineer
- Threat Intelligence Analyst
## Key Insight
Cybersecurity is not only about hacking. It includes monitoring , defending, engineering secure systems, and managing risk.
## Reflection
Given my background in finance and internal audit, roles such as SOC Analyst or Governance, Risk & Compliance align well with my analytical and risk-focused mindset.
